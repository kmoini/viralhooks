Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for coding creators?

Hooks that reveal a shortcut, debunk a career myth, or show a surprising income stat perform best. "The VS Code shortcut that saves me 2 hours a day" and "you don't need a CS degree to make $100K" consistently outperform generic tutorial content.

What coding content goes viral on TikTok?

Quick tips, salary reveals, "build this in 60 seconds" demos, junior dev career advice, and day-in-the-life content perform well. Content that demystifies tech for non-developers also gets huge reach beyond the dev community.

How do coding TikTokers grow their audience?

Niche down to a specific language, role, or outcome. "Python for beginners", "how to get your first dev job", or "freelance developer tips" grow faster than generic programming content. Bite-sized value posted consistently wins.
