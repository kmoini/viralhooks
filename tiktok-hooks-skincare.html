What skincare content goes viral on TikTok?

Product comparisons, ingredient deep-dives, routine reveals, "dermatologist secrets," and before/after transformation stories consistently go viral in the beauty space.

How do beauty creators grow fast on TikTok?

The fastest-growing beauty TikTokers post opinion-based content (controversial takes on popular products), use strong hooks that challenge conventional wisdom, and respond quickly to trending skincare topics.
