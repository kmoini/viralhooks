Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for small businesses?

Hooks sharing relatable struggles, surprising business lessons, or behind-the-scenes reality perform best. "POV: you're about to make the same mistake I did in year one" and "The free tool that replaced $500/month in software" consistently go viral.

Can small businesses actually grow on TikTok?

Yes — TikTok's algorithm gives small accounts the same reach as large ones based on content quality. Many small businesses have gone from zero to thousands of customers from a single viral video.

What small business content gets the most views on TikTok?

Day-in-the-life content, packing orders, business mistakes and lessons, income reveals, and "things I wish I knew before starting" videos perform exceptionally well.
