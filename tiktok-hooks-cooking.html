Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for food creators?

Hooks challenging a cooking assumption, revealing a chef secret, or promising a faster/better result perform best. "POV: you've been cooking garlic wrong your entire life" and "The one ingredient that makes everything taste restaurant-quality" consistently stop the scroll.

What cooking content goes viral on TikTok?

Quick recipes (under 60 seconds), cooking myth-busting, ingredient swap hacks, restaurant-quality-at-home challenges, and budget meal content consistently go viral.

How do food creators grow fast on TikTok?

Food TikTok rewards specificity. "Healthy 15-minute meals" outperforms "easy recipes." Strong hooks, satisfying visuals, and a clear ingredient-to-dish transformation drive the most shares.
