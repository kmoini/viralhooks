Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for mental health creators?

Hooks that name a specific, rarely-articulated experience perform best. "The anxiety symptom most people don't recognize" and "high-functioning anxiety looks like this" generate massive saves because they make people feel understood for the first time.

What mental health content goes viral on TikTok?

Content that names trauma responses, attachment patterns, burnout, and anxiety symptoms consistently goes viral. Practical coping tools and myth-busting content also perform well. Mental health content is saved more than almost any other category on TikTok.

How do mental health TikTokers grow their audience?

Focus on one area — anxiety, trauma, relationships, burnout — and use hooks that validate experiences people feel but can't name. Saves are the strongest algorithmic signal on TikTok, and mental health content that makes people feel seen gets saved at extremely high rates.
