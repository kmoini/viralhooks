Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for fitness creators?

The best fitness hooks reveal a surprising truth, challenge a common myth, or promise a fast result. "Stop doing crunches — do this instead" and "3 exercises that burn more fat than running" consistently outperform generic motivational content.

How often should fitness creators post on TikTok?

1–3 times per day is the sweet spot for fitness TikTok growth. A strong hook on every video matters more than volume — one great hook beats five mediocre ones.

What fitness niches get the most views on TikTok?

Weight loss, home workouts, gym tips, nutrition myths, and transformation content perform best. Specific sub-niches like "workouts for busy moms" or "gym tips for beginners" often outperform broad fitness content.
