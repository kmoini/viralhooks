Frequently asked questions

What is a TikTok hook?

A TikTok hook is the first 1–3 seconds of your video — the line or visual that makes someone stop scrolling. Strong hooks are the single biggest factor in TikTok reach because TikTok's algorithm weights watch time from second one.

What are the best TikTok hook formats?

The five most proven formats are: POV hooks (instant empathy), Number hooks (specific payoff), Bold Claim hooks (challenge assumptions), Question hooks (open cognitive loops), and Call-out hooks (speak directly to the viewer).

How long should a TikTok hook be?

3–10 words — short enough to read in under 2 seconds. Immediately communicate what the viewer gets, creating enough curiosity to watch on.

Do TikTok hooks work for all niches?

Yes. The same hook formulas work across fitness, finance, cooking, real estate, skincare, coaching, and small business. The formula stays constant; only the niche-specific words change.
