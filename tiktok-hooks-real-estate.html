Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for real estate agents?

Hooks that reveal insider secrets, expose buyer mistakes, or share surprising market data perform best. "The closing cost nobody tells you about" and "5 red flags buyers always ignore" consistently drive high engagement.

Should real estate agents be on TikTok?

Yes — TikTok has become a top platform for first-time homebuyers researching the process. Agents who post consistently report generating real leads directly from TikTok content.

What real estate content gets the most views on TikTok?

Home tours, "secrets agents won't tell you," market updates framed as surprises, and buyer/seller mistake videos go viral. Local market content performs well for lead generation.
