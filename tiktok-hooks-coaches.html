Frequently asked questions

What TikTok hooks work best for coaches?

Hooks that challenge coaching myths, reveal income realities, or expose client-getting mistakes perform best. "The $0 strategy that doubled my coaching income" and "Why your discovery call isn't converting" consistently drive high engagement.

How do coaches get clients from TikTok?

By consistently posting educational content that demonstrates expertise, using strong hooks to stop the scroll, and including a clear CTA (discovery call, free resource, DM) in every video.

What coaching content goes viral on TikTok?

Client result breakdowns, income transparency, "what I'd do differently" reflections, and myth-busting content about the coaching industry consistently go viral.
